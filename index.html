Rocket League Scorekeeper

Awesome app to keep track of stats during games. Seeks to allow more insight to teams than the default scoring and stats provide, such as: multi-pass assists, demos or bumps that result in goals, interceptions, errors in play, and many more!

Get started!

Start up a new game, review you previous logs, or check out stats for players or teams you've been tracking.
